Which Programming Language Are You?

Answer 3 quick questions to find out what programming language you would be or which you should use!


1

What type of subjects are your favorite to learn about?

  • Humanities - language, arts, social studies
  • STEM - science, technology, engineering, and math

2

Which of these traits would your friends describe you as?

  • Laid-back - you don't take things too seriously.
  • Organized - you like things to be in order and are somewhat of a perfectionist.

3

When working on something, do you prefer to take the easiest route or opt for a challenge?

  • Easy route - you like to "think smarter, not harder" and do what needs to get done.
  • Challenge - you prefer to go above and beyond the expectations to reach your full potential
